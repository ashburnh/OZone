Vice President

Duties

  • The Vice President shall be ready to fulfill the duties of the President in the event that he is not present.
  • He shall oversee all standing committees and all non-standing committees.
  • He shall assist the President in all day-to-day monitoring of the brotherhood.
  • He shall help facilitate all officer retreats and transitions.
  • He shall act as President of the executive board.
  • He shall oversee rush and make sure we recruit the finest gentlemen at this university.
  • He shall publicly support the President, creating a united front as far as leading the brotherhood goes, but challenge him to ensure that ethics are never compromised and there is no abuse of executive power.
