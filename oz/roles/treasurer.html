Treasurer

Duties

  • The Treasurer shall oversee the budgetary committee and make sure the Fraternity has a budget for every semester to ensure effective distribution of funds.
  • He shall present a budget for the following semester at the final chapter of the current semester to be approved by the fraternity.
  • He shall collect and manage fraternity dues.
  • He shall maintain a posted list of all brotherâ€™s outstanding balances.
  • He shall facilitate purchases made on behalf of the Fraternity.
  • He shall maintain a log of all receipts for purchases made for the Fraternity.
  • He shall be present at all events where fraternity money is being spent.
  • Treasurer has the power to make payment plans with individual brothers who have issues paying full dues by the initial due date. this can be based on good standing, financial issues, and brotherhood commitment. Amount paid based on treasurers discretion.
    • Payment plan can be revisited by Treasurer if payments are not on time and brotherhood commitments after plan implemented.
    • Three interval payments will be established for anyone following the payment plan, where a third of dues will be due without exceptions at each interval. Failure to pay in this timely fashion can result in loss of favors, exclusion from brotherhood events and if all three interval payments are missed becoming an inactive brother till the semester debt is paid in full.
