Pledge Master(s)

Duties

  • The Pledge Masters shall see that all brothers are present for mandatory initiation functions.
  • They shall supervise and administer all initiation events.
  • They shall educate the pledges on the history of OZ.
  • They shall ensure the safety of their pledge class.
  • They shall act as the liaison between the pledges and the brothers.
  • They shall ensure that the pledges develop as individuals and are fit to be in the brotherhood by the end of their pledge process.
  • He shall be chairman of the Rush committee consisting of the pms, the social chair, and the vice president.
  • He shall keep in contact with the rushees.
  • He shall maintain a contact list for all active rushees for public viewing.
