President

Duties

  • The President shall oversee all brotherhood day-to-day operations.
  • He shall be responsible for initiating action outside of the normal operating procedures of the brotherhood, should a situation arise that requires such action.
  • He shall ensure that the brotherhood conducts brotherhood building activities at least once a month.
  • He shall organize and appoint chairmen to all non-standing committees, and at the time of inception of such committees shall state the objectives to be fulfilled.
  • He shall dissolve all such committees on the completion of their objectives.
  • He shall be the face of the brotherhood and represent it with dignity.
