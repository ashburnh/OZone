Secretary

Duties

  • The Secretary shall take quality notes at every chapter meeting, and make them available to the entire brotherhood.
  • He shall track attendance at every chapter meeting.
  • He shall maintain an organized calendar of all brotherhood-sanctioned events.
  • He shall maintain a current contact list for all brothers.
  • He shall manage brotherhood-wide communication.
